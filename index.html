One Stop Solution For All Your Needs

Academics Management
Streams/Departments

Students stay updated with the necessary information regarding their respective departments/streams and modifications done by the admins/teachers. ,

Course and Batch

Students can easily access their batch details and multiple course details efficiently through the software that helps in reducing their efforts and increasing productivity.

Lesson Planning

Lesson planning is the day to day activity performed by the teacher and skillfully accessed by students for their respective classes that helps them to prepare the books and material required.

Circular

Through Circulars, students and parents can know about the upcoming events/functions/important things issued by the school authority that keeps them updated.

Certificate

Certificates of different activities like Drawing/Rank-holder/Sports-competition/Training and many more issued by teachers, even customized certificates issued for studies unique cases.

Assignment and Notes

Students can view Assignments & Notes given by teachers to their respective classes. Students and parents get a notification on their mobile application for the assignment/note received from the school.

Time Table

Admin/teachers can generate, maintain and make changes in the schedule of their respective classes allowing students to stay updated on the coming weekly learning plan.


Scholarship

Our module shows information about Scholarships granted to students that help the school automization of the function by endowing the students with their fees and higher education.

CLASSWORK & HOMEWORK

Students can view classwork & homework given by teachers to their respective classes. Students and parents get a notification on their mobile application for the classwork/homework received from the school.

Student Management
Online Admission

Our module helps the school to manage online admission by reducing unwanted data. It allows candidates to Fill out an Application form from Anywhere and Anytime. It helps in cost savings for the institute.

Time Table management

Teachers’ can easily prepare, maintain and update timetables as per the requirement in subject, food and exam schedules with our system to make it easy for students and stay updated on a daily/weekly basis.

Task Management

Teachers/authorities can create and allocate different task names with proper and accurate descriptions to the students as they can complete them according to their priority and the assigned date.

Event Management

An authority person can maintain and update different events planning relating to schools/colleges/universities like festivals, sports days, drawing competitions and other activities for students.

Canteen Management

Canteen management updates students’ regarding the weekly meal plan, food inventory records and the number of meals consumed with secure and speedy cashless transactions.

Student Tracking

Through Student Tracking, it becomes leisurely for admin/teachers to scan, detect and track a particular student on the school premises via ID cards and warn their parents through messages.

Scholarship

Our module updates the authorities about scholarships granted to the students for academic achievements or financial aid for students for their higher education. The institute can issue the Refund in Terms of the Scholarship Program.

Student Performance

Management can prepare Student Performance reports class-wise/section-wise efficiently. It makes it simple for school authorities and parents to keep track of students.

Email & Notification & Chat

Day-to-day noteworthy conversations like SMS and email between Principle/teachers and students are sent and can be received anywhere, anytime through our ERP software.


Examination Management
Manual Examination

In Manual Examination, the admin can plan, design and schedule the exam paper and allocate ranks to the students according to their performance.


Question Bank

Admin can create a question bank list for all subjects. Teachers / Admin can upload question banks, exam papers and solutions. It makes it simple for the students as they can view and download them from the system anytime when they need them.

Online Examination

The Online Examination feature makes it simple and saves time for teachers/admin as they can create subjective and descriptive questions for a final exam or a surprise test. It also provides instant results to the students and in the admin records.

Examination Time-Table

Admins can create examination timetables for weekly, monthly and yearly exams and can operate with proper planning and scheduling.


Set Grading/ Ranking Levels

The online examination module will help calculate grading/ranking levels according to the student exam performance. The student will get fast results with a better evaluation process.

Exam Result

Through this module, the admin/teachers can create, manage, and efficiently declare the online results of every individual student.


Supervisor and Examiner Management

Manage the list of exams conducted by the supervisor and examiner and track and view examiner reports to check the subject wise paper. Admin can assign supervisors and examiners according to their field of work and speciality.

Exam Report

This module helps admin/teachers design and manage exam reports of each student and generate different types of Reports containing students' monthly and yearly activities and performance evolution.


Class Designation

They automatically and effectively assigned a ranking level to the individual class according to their performance.




Payroll & FinanceManagement
Salary Setting

The admin can manage employee salary, with employee name, designation, pay head and amount paid to the employee as a salary and payroll activities of the institute.

Pay Head

Display and supervise master data type with the feature to add the different pay head, pay head name and method of payment by admin.

Pay Type

Display the entries of payment type names, and the admin can also add other payment types in this module if needed by the authority.

Generate Pay slip

Generate and manage the payslip for the additional months /years reports. It can also generate dynamic salary slips with a designation and send them to the employees.

Account management

Our module manages and supervises bank/cash transactions, expenses, trial balance, balance sheet and journal vouchers.

Fees Management

Admin can handle and manage all the fee related institutional activities with this module resulting in highly accurate data
.

Reports

This module helps the admin to manage school/college/institute Journal books, Cash/Bank books, General Ledger, Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, and Profit & Loss reports.

HR Management

HR can oversee the list of activities like Recruiting, Job posting, Analysis of work, planning and controlling resources and managing organization employees.

Masters

Finance management can operate and handle different types of Masters like Account Master, Expense Master, Bank/Cash Master, Tax Master etc.

Transportation/Library& Hostel Management
Vehicle, Driver Details

Our module displays a list of the vehicle number, max seat allocation, and insurance renewal dates and handles various driver contact numbers and details.

Vehicle Tracking

Our module helps admin/teachers and students/parents to track the vehicles through an RFID system.


Route Details

Our module displays area wise allocated route details and the driver names. It will automatically send the push notification to the parents about the Route update if there is any change in the daily bus route.

Destination and Fees

Our module creates and manages the schedule for the students and employees and shows details of the Fee structure. Admin can add, change no of destinations and parents/students can view destination from pick up point to drop point.

Transportation Allocation

The admin can manage to allocate transportation efficiently with start and end dates. It will provide the allocation of all institute transport vehicles.


Hostel Details

The admin can regularly view and check the Hostel details like the list of hostel types, No. of students details, No of students living in the hostel, fee structure and Hostel canteen menu from the system and mobile app.

Hostel Canteen

The admin can handle the canteen menu and caterer details, update the food menu, maintain coupon records and deliver regular updates to all students/teachers/parents from the system and mobile app.

Book Category

An authorized person can view, manage and handle the list of books category to keep track of all the books that can be issued.


Book issue and Return

The admin effectively tracks and manages teachers' and students' book issues list and book return process. They know the no of book issue and return past and the current period.
